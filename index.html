How it works

01

Create a room

Type any room name — something like fridaynight — and hit join. No account needed.

02

Share with a friend

Tell your friend the room name. They open the site, type the same name, and they're in.

03

Paste a video link

Drop in a YouTube URL or a Google Drive share link. The video loads for everyone in the room.

04

Watch in sync

Play, pause, and seek — everything stays in sync. Chat alongside the video in real time.
